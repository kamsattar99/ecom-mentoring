## 7. FAQ SECTION

**Eyebrow:** FAQs

**Headline:**
Questions Before You *Apply?*

**Subtext:**
Before you apply, here are the questions most serious founders ask about the programme, the AI integration, and whether it is the right fit.

**FAQs:**

1. **Is this only for people who already know AI?**
   No. The programme is designed to teach you how to use AI properly inside ecommerce — from scratch. You do not need technical skills or prior AI experience. We show you the exact workflows.

2. **Do I need technical skills to join?**
   No. You do not need coding, design or technical experience. The AI tools and workflows we teach are practical and built for ecommerce operators, not developers.

3. **How is this different from watching free YouTube videos?**
   YouTube gives you information. This gives you implementation. You get personalised feedback, direct mentor access, AI workflows built for your business, and accountability to actually execute — not just consume content.

4. **How is the new AI version different from the old mentorship?**
   The entire curriculum has been rebuilt around AI-assisted ecommerce execution. Every stage — from product research to scaling — now includes AI workflows, tools and systems that did not exist in the previous version.

5. **Will AI build the business for me?**
   No. AI gives you speed and leverage, but you still need to execute, test and make decisions. The mentorship helps you use AI properly while building real skills — not just outsource everything to a chatbot.

6. **Do you guarantee results?**
   No. Results depend on your product selection, budget, execution, market conditions and consistency. What we guarantee is structured guidance, direct feedback and proven systems — the outcomes depend on your implementation.

7. **How much time do I need to commit each week?**
   The more consistently you implement, the faster you will progress. As a guideline, you should be able to commit focused weekly time to research, store work, creative testing, calls and execution.

8. **Is this for beginners or existing store owners?**
   Both. The programme is structured to guide complete beginners through the foundations, while also helping existing store owners upgrade their systems, use AI properly and scale with better structure.

9. **What happens after I apply?**
   Applications are reviewed within 48 hours. If it looks like a good fit, you will be invited to a short call to discuss your goals, current situation and whether the programme is right for you. No pressure.

10. **Is this still dropshipping or a full ecommerce brand mentorship?**
    Both. Whether you are starting with dropshipping or building a branded store, the systems, AI workflows and mentorship apply. The goal is to help you build a real ecommerce business — not just run a temporary product store.
